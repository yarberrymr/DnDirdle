Dungeons and Dirdles

How to Play

The monsters that are used in this game come from part of the DnD 5e SRD.
Some monsters in this game are not in the main monster manuals.

The goal is to guess which random monster has been chosen by the game.
You can achieve this by looking at the type, HP, size, and alignment of the
monsters you have guessed.
